													<b>Chingay: 50 Years and Counting</b><br><br>

The Chingay parade would be held at Jewel Changi Airport on 12 February 2022. Chairman of Chingay50 Committee and Group Director of PA Kia Siang Wei, said the parade would showcase Chingay’s 50-year journey since it started in 1973. He hoped Singaporeans would resonate with the Chingay spirit and be encouraged to pursue their passions, unleash their potential and fulfil their aspirations.

This year's We Got Talent event received a resounding response with more than 600 people. The public participated in the event by filming a one minute video to share their talents. Out of the multitude of entries, our winner, Joshua Low was crowned Chingay Community Star 2022 for his skilled saxophone performance. At just 13 years old, his rendition of Easy On Me by Adele wowed us all.
