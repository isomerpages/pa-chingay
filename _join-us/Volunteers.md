<h1><strong>Volunteer Recruitment for Chingay 2026 is back!</strong></h1>
<p>We would like to invite you to join us as volunteers to support the parade
and contribute to the positive experiences of people from all walks of
life.</p>
<p>Alternatively, we are on a lookout for performing groups that can light
up the Chingay Parade and electrify the crowd with your energy, talent,
and never-ending passion! If you and your group are interested, you may
register via: <a href="https://form.gov.sg/64a2386e773e020011928553" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Join Us!</a>
</p>
<h2><strong>Pre-Requisites for Volunteer Application</strong></h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>General Volunteers - 15 years old and above</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>EXCO / Volunteer Leaders - 17 years old and above</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Able to commit for all the sessions stated in the application form</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2><strong>Appreciation for All Volunteers</strong></h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Meals will be provided on key dates of involvement</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Chingay Volunteer T-shirt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Certificate of Appreciation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Letter of acknowledgement of your participation (VIA letter) upon request</p>
</li>
</ol>
